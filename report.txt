PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

Why 100% Pair Identification but 90.7% Event Recall?
----------------------------------------------------
The model always ranks the true π⁰ pair as the highest-scoring candidate 
(100% ranking accuracy). However, for 9.3% of π⁰ events, the confidence score 
falls below the 0.5 threshold:

• The true π⁰ pair is correctly identified as the best candidate
• But its probability score is low (typically 0.3-0.5 range)
• These events typically involve π⁰ decays with challenging kinematics:
  - Low photon energies
  - Highly asymmetric decays
  - Small opening angles
  - Photons close to other background photons
